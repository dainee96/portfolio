Background
About Me

I'm an AI/ML Engineer based in Seoul with 4+ years of experience turning medical imaging research into production-ready clinical tools. I work across the full stack — from model architecture and training to cloud pipeline engineering and international clinical validation.

My work spans CT, MRI, and X-ray modalities across cardiovascular, oncology, and infectious disease domains. I've delivered AI solutions under Korea's national healthcare R&D programs (Ministry of Health & Welfare, NIPA) and validated models in real-world clinical settings including National University Hospital in Singapore.

I hold an M.S. in Convergence Software from Hallym University, have co-authored 7 SCI-level papers, and hold 3 international patents (KR/EU/US) in medical imaging AI.
